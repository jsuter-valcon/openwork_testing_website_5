Corporate social responsibility

We are The Openwork Partnership

Our vision is to be the leading financial advice company in the UK: by better outcomes for more clients, by reputation and by growth.

At the heart of our business there are over 4,200 financial advisers based in nearly 700 firms and supported by 600 colleagues in our central teams. Together we are a partnership and financial advice is what we do - it’s our shared history, our DNA.

Whether you are planning a career with us, growing your business, protecting your family or building a retirement plan……our purpose is to make it easier for you to face your future with confidence and optimism.

The Openwork Partnership family includes…

Omnis Investments

The Omnis’ fund range is available exclusively to The Openwork Partnership advisers and clients and offers a full range of equity, fixed interest, multi asset, multi-manager and income funds.

2plan Wealth Management

Our national independent advisory firm provides professional financial advice through its over 280 advisers to over 80,000 clients.

Visit Website

Owl Financial

A nationwide network of well over a thousand advisers with a focus on helping clients protect what is most important to them. Their expertise includes income protection, life insurance, critical illness and accident protection cover.

The Openwork Foundation
