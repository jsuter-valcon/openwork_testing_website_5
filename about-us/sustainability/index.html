Sustainability

When we refer to sustainability, it incorporates four main groups: Our clients, our communities, our Partnership (advisers and colleagues), and the planet. We are committed to safeguarding and improving the lives of these groups. This commitment is deeply embedded in our values - the basis of how we conduct ourselves, our business and our role within the community.

Within The Openwork Partnership, we have a working group, dedicated to furthering our sustainability efforts.

Being a responsible business

Considering what we can do in our head office to be a better company, encompassing how we care for our colleagues (DWI), and how we hold ourselves accountable through our governance.

A fundamental part of this is supporting the Openwork Foundation, to change lives and make a difference to vulnerable communities.
